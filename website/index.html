Powerful Features

Everything you need to run a successful car rental business

Vehicle Management

Track all your vehicles with detailed information including make, model, year, license plate, and maintenance history.

Customer Management

Manage customer information, rental history, and loyalty programs with our comprehensive customer database.

Rental Scheduling

Visual calendar interface to manage rentals, check availability, and schedule pickups and returns.

Contract Generation

Generate professional rental contracts with company branding and automatic PDF creation.

Analytics & Reports

Comprehensive analytics dashboard with revenue tracking, vehicle performance, and business insights.

Multi-Language Support

Full support for English, Arabic, and French with RTL layout support for Arabic text.

Smart Notifications

Automated reminders for rental pickups, returns, maintenance schedules, and license renewals.

Offline Operation

Work completely offline with local data storage. No internet connection required for daily operations.
